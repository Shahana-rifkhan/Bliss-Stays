Bliss Stays Frequently Asked Questions

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 15:00 onwards, and check-out is by 11:00. Early check-in or late check-out is available for £15 per hour, subject to availability. Please contact us in advance to arrange.

How does the check-in process work?

Check-in is usually self-service via a lockbox or smart lock. You'll receive all access details and instructions before your arrival via WhatsApp.

Can I cancel or change my booking after it's made?

Yes, cancellation and change policies vary depending on your booking type. Please refer to your confirmation email or contact us for details.

Do I need to provide ID or anything else to check in?

Yes, a valid ID with address proof is required at check-in. We may also ask for your booking confirmation to verify your reservation.

Can I make a group booking or book multiple apartments?

Absolutely! Group bookings are welcome and subject to availability. Please contact us directly for assistance with larger bookings.

Do you offer housekeeping during my stay?

Housekeeping services vary by length of stay and property. Additional cleaning can often be arranged upon request for an extra fee.

Are linens, towels, and toiletries provided?

Yes, we provide fresh linens, towels, and basic toiletries for your stay. If you need extras, please let us know in advance, requests made after arrival may incur an additional charge.

Is there a kitchen or kitchenette in the apartment?

All of our apartments come with a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette. You’ll find essentials like a stove, fridge, and basic utensils.

Is smoking allowed in the apartment or building?

All our apartments are strictly non-smoking. Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor areas, if available.

Are pets allowed in the apartment?

Pets are not allowed unless explicitly mentioned in the listing. Please check with us before booking if you plan to bring a pet.

Is WiFi included, and how fast is it?

Yes, complimentary WiFi is available at all properties. Speed and reliability may vary by location.

Is the apartment heated or air-conditioned?

All our apartments come with heating for year-round comfort. Air conditioning units are not standard but can be provided during summer for an additional charge. Please let us know in advance if required.

Do you offer parking on-site or nearby?

Parking options vary by location. Some apartments offer on-site parking or nearby public parking. Please contact us for details.

Is there lift access in the building?

Our properties do not have lifts, which is common in many UK buildings. Please keep this in mind, especially if mobility is a concern.

Are there laundry facilities available?

Yes, all our apartments have laundry facilities, with a washer and dryer available for your convenience during your stay.

Where are your apartments located?

Our apartments are in central, well-connected locations across the city. Each listing includes detailed location information and nearby landmarks.

What’s the best way to get to the apartment from the airport or station?

You can reach the apartment by taxi, Uber, or public transport such as trains or buses. We’re happy to provide detailed directions and travel tips based on your arrival point if requested.

Are there restaurants, cafes, or supermarkets nearby?

Yes, our apartments are in well-connected areas with restaurants, cafes, and supermarkets nearby. Options may vary, but essentials are usually within walking distance.

Is the neighbourhood safe?

Yes, our apartments are located in family-friendly neighbourhoods that are generally safe. However, as with any city, we recommend being mindful of your belongings.

Can I store luggage before check-in or after check-out?

Yes, luggage drop-off is available for £25, subject to availability. This service is usually accommodated between 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM. Please contact us in advance to arrange.
